I am a PhD student at the University of California, Berkeley in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science department.

My research focuses on Embedded Systems. I am part of Lab11 and work with Professor Prabal Dutta.
I strongly believe in open-access to research, and you can find software and hardware for all of my projects on Github.
